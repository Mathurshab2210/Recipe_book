<span id="p1">For Sweet Lassi</span> <br>
▢2 cups Curd – cold and fresh (yogurt) <br>
▢1 cup water or milk – cold or at room temperature <br>
▢4 to 5 tablespoons sugar or add as per taste <br>
▢1 teaspoon cardamom powder or 8 to 10 green  cardamoms or 1 tablespoon rose water<br>
▢10 to 12 saffron strands – optional <br>
▢6 to 8 ice cubes – optional <br>
▢1 to 2 tablespoons chopped nuts – cashews, almonds, pistachios, optional <br>
<span id="p1">For Salted Lassi</span> <br>
▢2.5 cups Curd – cold and fresh (yogurt) <br>
▢1 cup water – cold or at room temperature <br>
▢1 teaspoon black salt or regular salt or edible rock salt, as required <br>
▢1.5 teaspoons roasted cumin powder <br>
▢6 to 7 mint leaves <br>
For Masala Lassi <br>
▢2 cups Curd – cold and fresh (yogurt) <br>
▢3 to 4 green cardamoms – seeds crushed  <br>in mortar and husks removed <br>
▢¼ teaspoon crushed black pepper <br>
▢1 to 2 pinches grated nutmeg or ground nutmeg <br>
▢2 tablespoons chopped almonds or cashews or pistachios <br>
▢10 to 12 saffron strands – optional <br>
▢5 to 6 tablespoons chopped palm sugar (palm jaggery) or jaggery or sugar <br>
▢⅓ to ½ cup water – cold or at room temperature, add as required <br>
