<p id="para"><span id="p1">veggie patties preparation:</span> <br>
firstly, steam ½ carrot, 6 gobi, 4 beans, 2 tbsp sweet corn, 2 tbsp peas and ¼ tsp salt. <br>
steam for 10 minutes on medium flame. <br>
transfer the steamed vegetables to large mixing bowl. <br>
also, add 3 boiled and mashed potato.
further, add ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp chaat masala and ½ tsp salt. <br>
add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well combining all the spices well.
furthermore add ¼ cup bread crumbs and mix well. breadcrumbs help to absorb moisture from vegetables. <br>
prepare a slurry by mixing 2 tbsp cornflour, ¼ cup maida and ½ cup water in a small bowl. <br>
prepare ball sized patties and dip in maida slurry.
coat with panko breadcrumbs uniformly. <br>
shallow fry in hot oil keeping the flame on medium.
fry on both sides, until it turns golden brown. drain off and keep aside. <br>
<span id="p1">burger preparation: </span><br>
firstly, in a small bowl take 3 tbsp eggless mayonnaise, 2 tbsp tomato sauce and 1 tsp chilli sauce. <br>
mix well-forming burger sauce.
spread a tbsp of prepared burger sauce on the bun.
place a few slices of tomato, cucumber and jalapeno. <br>
sprinkle salt and pepper slightly.
layer with a cheese slice. <br>
further place prepared patties and cover with another part of the bun. <br>
finally, enjoy veggie burger with chips and salad.
</p>
